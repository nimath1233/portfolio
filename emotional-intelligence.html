I learned that Daniel Goleman's framework divides EQ into five components, which I want to apply to myself:

1. Self-Awareness

I learned this is my ability to recognize and understand my own moods, emotions, and drives, as well as their effect on my peers, helping me identify my emotional triggers.

2. Self-Regulation

I realized this is my capacity to control or redirect disruptive impulses and think before acting, which is key to managing my stress.

3. Motivation

I learned to cultivate an internal drive to pursue goals with energy and persistence, relying on self-motivation and delaying gratification.

4. Empathy

I realized this is my ability to understand the emotional makeup of others and treat them with respect, which is crucial for my teamwork.

5. Social Skills

The session showed me that proficiency in managing relationships and building networks will help me build rapport, communicate, and manage conflict.
